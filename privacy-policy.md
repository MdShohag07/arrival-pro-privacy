# Privacy Policy — Arrival Pro

Arrival Pro ("the extension") helps authorised users process product arrivals on Miinto's partner portal (proxy-arrival.miinto.net).

## What the extension handles
- Google account email: read once via Chrome's identity.email API only to verify the user is on the approved-users list (access control). If the optional device-lock feature is enabled, it is sent only to the account owner's own server to enforce one device per user. It is never sold, shared, or used for advertising.
- Page data on proxy-arrival.miinto.net: Style IDs, sizes, brand and category are read from the page only to perform the automation, and are stored locally in the browser (chrome.storage.local).
- Usage counts: numbers of products submitted/rejected and submitted Style IDs are stored locally to power the in-extension dashboard.

## What we do not do
- We do not collect, transmit, or store personal data on our own servers.
- We do not use analytics, tracking, or advertising.
- We do not sell or share any data with third parties.
- All data stays on the user's device, except the optional device-lock check, which is sent only to a server the account owner deploys and controls.

## Data retention
All data is stored locally and can be cleared at any time from the extension (Reset/Clear) or by removing the extension.
